LOK SABHA

LOK SABHA simulates the lower house of parliament, focusing on legislative processes, policy-making, and democratic debates.Here, delegates will experience the intricacies of parliamentary democracy.

Desciption

Lok Sabha stands as the battleground of minds and motives, ideas forged and tested in the heat of debate. Political leaders go head-to-head, determined to win over the room with sharp words and sharper minds. Where intellect meets intensity, where preparation meets pressure.

From heated exchanges to moments of quiet conviction, every delegate in Lok Sabha walks the tightrope between strategy and spontaneity. Every point made is a step toward shaping opinions and steering the direction of the discussion.

This is where emerging leaders elevate their voices, challenge perspectives, and engage deeply with nuanced subjects. This is where every word is influential, and every debate shapes the leaders of tomorrow.

This is the Lok Sabha.
