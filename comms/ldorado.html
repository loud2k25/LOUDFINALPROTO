L'DORADO

L'DORADO is an innovative committee exploring mythical and legendary themes in international diplomacy. Delegates will engage in creative debates, blending fantasy elements with real-world policy discussions.

Rules

  1. To speak in the debate, one must raise his or her assigned token or placard.
  2. The session will be carried out in sequence in all 6 Pillars. Discussion of one pillar should be completed before proceeding to the next.
  3. After the discussion on each pillar, the host will present a ‘What If?’ scenario. This situation will be used to check the feasibility of the offered agreements, and the floor will be reopened to discuss this issue to eliminate such shortcomings.
  4. The parties (who operate within their own groups) can engage in in-house negotiations, i.e. make alliances, trade-offs, and negotiate to ensure that their motions are voted on.
  5. All such agreements should be officially reported to the hosts. Deals that have not been reported will be disregarded, and points will not be awarded.
  6. The final act is the signing of the final Utopian Charter, which is to be done together by all participants.

Description

There is a city that exists only in the space between ideals and action. A place of pure potential, waiting for its architects. This is not a search for lost gold, but the forging of a new one.

Fifty souls are summoned to the table. Five competing visions for humanity. One impossible task: to weave a new social fabric from the six strings—Power, Wealth, Laws, Defence, Culture and Future. Here, every word is a brick, every alliance a cornerstone. You will debate, negotiate and confront the ultimate test: whether perfection can survive human nature.

The path is paved with "what ifs." Each decision echoes into a future you must face. Corruption, betrayal and collapse are not endpoints—they are tests.

This is the experiment. The golden dream is ready to be drawn. Will your signature be upon it?
